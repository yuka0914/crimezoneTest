What's Your Stereotype of Japan?

When you think about Japan, what will come up in your mind?

Sushi? Tech? or beautiful Mt. Fuji?

All the images you have right now will be completely destroyed in a few mins... Enjoy!

Sushi.

Sushi definitely is one of the most famous and popular Japanese food in the world.

But, do you know Japanese people don't actually eat Sushi that often?

see the FACT
Japanese Don't Eat Sushi Often.

According to a survey by Penpal service, only about 25% of Japanese eat sushi more than once per month.

 

Another 25% enjoys it just once a month and 35% once in two to four months.

The remaining 13% eat sushi even less frequently, and surprisingly, 3% responded to never eat sushi at all.

 

Perheps, you guys eat more Sushi than we Japanese do.

High-Tech.

Japan was ranked "the most powerful brand" out of 118 countries, because of their own high-tech product.

But, is Japan really a high-tech country?

see the FACT
Japan isn't a High-Tech Country.

According to BBC News, 87.5% of Japanese businessmen answered "a fax machine is a crucial business tool".

 

Also a survey by Japan minister of internal affairs and communications shows that 46% of Japanese population still use fax machine.

 

This number is more than tablet users's population!

Mt. Fuji

The highest mountain in Japan with a beautiful cone shape. Without doubt, Mt. Fuji is the world-famous symbol of Japan.

But, do you know this mountain is famous for another reason?

see the FACT
Mt. Fuji is a "Suicide Forest".

Japan has one of the highest suicide rate among developed nations.

Accoring to OECD, Japan ranked on Top 5 highest suicide rate in 2012.

Everyday, 90 people killed themselves and it means 1 person is ending their life in every 16 mins in Japan.

"Aogigahara", 35-square-kilometre "Sea of Trees" that lies at the northwest base of Mt. Fuji is known for "Suicide Forest".

Every year, 100 dead body are found there.
